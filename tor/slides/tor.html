Tor: The onion Router

Allows to hide data leaked by TCP/IP protocol

What is it?

  • Protocol for anonymous connections over internet, based on a semicentralized network.
  • Free implementation (GPL) of the protocol.
  • Network of ~6000 voluntary nodes (open).
  • Organization that manages it all.

Who uses it?

About de 1.500.000 users

  • Law enforcement.
  • Investigative journalists.
  • Activists.
  • Whistleblowers.
  • Victims of domestic violence/bullying/harassment...
  • People in regimes that censor or otherwise prosecute free speach.
  • People that cares about privacy.
  • Servicies behind de NAT, local networks...
  • ...

Warnings

  • Does not encrypt traffic outside Tor network
  • Does not hide the fact that Tor is being used (*)
  • Not perfect, there have been attacks, but:
  • Attacks are hard, and mostly non targeted
  • Overall it is the best option nowadays

(*) Bridges

  • Non published entry nodes
  • Can obfuscate the fact that Tor is being used
  • Limited and selective publication
  • A few come by default
  • Others can be asked for via web
  • By email
  • In person
